Choose Your Adventure: Game Modes

How will you play? The choice is yours.

Survival

Gather resources, craft tools, build shelter, and manage your hunger. At night, monsters come out. Can you survive the dark?

Creative

Your imagination is the only limit. With unlimited resources and the ability to fly, you can build anything you dream of.

Hardcore

For the ultimate challenge. The difficulty is locked on hard, and death is permanent. This is not for the faint of heart.

Adventure

Play on custom maps created by others. Interact with the world but without the ability to freely place or destroy blocks.

A Constantly Evolving Canvas

The world of Minecraft never stops growing.

The Update Aquatic (1.13)

Dived deep into the oceans, adding vibrant coral reefs, mysterious shipwrecks, dolphins, and new dangers lurking in the depths.

The Nether Update (1.16)

Completely revamped the fiery Nether dimension, introducing new biomes like the Crimson Forest, new mobs like Piglins, and the powerful Netherite.

Caves & Cliffs (1.18)

Redefined the very foundation of the world with sprawling cave systems, lush caverns, and towering mountains, making exploration more breathtaking than ever.
